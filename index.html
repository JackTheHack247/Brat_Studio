Game Features

Everything you can do in the Isle of Man

01

Racing

Compete in Forza-style races across the island. From street circuits to the legendary Isle of Man TT course, every race is a new challenge.

02

Isle of Man TT

Take on the world's most dangerous race. Experience the TT course with authentic physics and breathtaking speed.

03

Sailing & Piracy

Set sail around the island's coast. Hunt for treasure, engage in naval combat, and live the pirate life inspired by Sea of Thieves.

04

Exploration

Discover hidden locations, secret paths, and breathtaking vistas. The Isle of Man is yours to explore on land and sea.

05

Vehicle Variety

From motorcycles to supercars, sailboats to pirate ships - choose your vehicle and style of play.

06

Dynamic Weather

Experience the Isle of Man's legendary weather. Rain, fog, and changing conditions affect every race and adventure.
